What are Crypto Airdrops?

Crypto airdrops are free distributions of cryptocurrency tokens or coins to wallet addresses. Projects use airdrops for marketing, community building, and decentralization of token ownership. AirdropAlpha helps you identify legitimate opportunities with real value potential.
