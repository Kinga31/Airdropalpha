Legit Airdrop Checklist (Avoid Scams)

A practical checklist to verify if an airdrop is legit, avoid phishing, and protect your wallet while farming new airdrops and upcoming rewards.

What you should do (fast)

  1. Pick 3–5 projects from the main list and focus on consistency.
  2. Use a dedicated wallet for airdrops and never share private keys.
  3. Follow official links only (site + X + Discord) before doing tasks.
  4. Track tasks + dates in one note to avoid missing eligibility.

How we define a “legit” airdrop

  • Clear official channels + consistent announcements.
  • No “send funds to claim” requirement.
  • Reasonable tasks (social / testnet / usage) with transparent rules.
  • Healthy community signals (not pure bot noise).

FAQ

Do I need to pay? Legit airdrops should not require payments to claim.

How long does it take? Most rewards are announced weeks/months later.

Can I do many projects? Yes, but consistency beats quantity.
